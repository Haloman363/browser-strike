<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 2: TSL Muzzle Flash Compute Logic</name>
  <files>src/systems/FXSystem.js</files>
  <action>
    1. Implement TSL `compute()` nodes in `FXSystem`:
       - `computeSpawn`: Overwrites a slice of the storage buffer with initial muzzle position, randomized velocity (cone shape), and randomized life duration.
       - `computeUpdate`: Updates position by velocity * deltaTime, adds gravity (downward acceleration), and applies air drag. Increments age and marks dead particles.
    2. Use `renderer.compute(computeUpdate)` in the `update()` loop of FXSystem.
    3. Ensure `computeSpawn` is dispatched when `weapon:fired` occurs.
  </action>
