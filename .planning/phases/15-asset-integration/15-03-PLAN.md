# Phase 15: Asset Integration - Plan 03: High-Fidelity Arsenal Expansion

**Objective:** Complete the high-fidelity weapon arsenal by integrating the AWP and Five-SeveN external models.

## Key Goals
1. **AWP Integration**: Map the AWP OBJ/MTL assets to the `AWP` weapon key.
2. **Five-SeveN Integration**: Map the Five-SeveN OBJ/MTL assets to the `FIVESEVEN` weapon key (need to ensure this key exists or map to P250).
3. **Scope Fidelity**: Ensure the AWP model includes its high-res scope texture for improved visual quality during aiming.
4. **Final Refinement**: Audit all integrated models for scale consistency and texture alignment.
