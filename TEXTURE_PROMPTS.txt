# Browser Strike Texture Generation Guide

Use the following prompts in your image generator (like Google AI Studio, Midjourney, or DALL-E) to create high-quality seamless textures for the environment.

## 1. Sand Texture (Ground)
Prompt: Fine desert sand texture, warm beige and tan colors, subtle dunes and ripple patterns, detailed grains, seamless tiling, top-down orthographic view, high resolution.
Save as: sand.png

## 2. Wall Texture (Buildings/Obstacles)
Prompt: Weathered tan brick wall texture, old sun-bleached stone, subtle mortar lines, rough architectural surface, seamless tiling, flat front-on view, 4k detail.
Save as: wall.png

## 3. Concrete Texture (Industrial areas)
Prompt: Rough grey industrial concrete texture, subtle cracks and weathering, dark spots and noise, weathered cement surface, seamless tiling, flat top-down view.
Save as: concrete.png

## 4. Glove Texture (Player Hands)
Prompt: Tactical combat glove fabric texture, dark charcoal grey leather and nylon weave, hexagonal reinforcement patterns, rugged military material, seamless tiling.
Save as: glove.png

---

### Instructions for Installation:

1. Generate the images based on the prompts above.
2. Save or convert each image to the .png format.
3. Place the files in the following directory in your project:
   /mnt/c/Users/Jaymes/Github Repos/browser-strike/assets/textures/

### File Names (Case Sensitive):
- assets/textures/sand.png
- assets/textures/wall.png
- assets/textures/concrete.png
- assets/textures/glove.png

Once these files are in place, the game's `TextureGenerator.js` will automatically detect them on the next reload and swap out the simple procedural colors for your high-quality textures!
