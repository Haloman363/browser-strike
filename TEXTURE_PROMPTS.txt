# Browser Strike Texture Generation Guide (Remaining Assets)

Use the following prompts in your image generator (like Google AI Studio, Midjourney, or DALL-E) to create high-quality seamless textures for the remaining weapons and characters.

## --- WEAPONS & ITEMS ---

## 1. Gun Metal / Steel Texture
Prompt: Brushed gunmetal steel texture, dark charcoal grey, subtle metallic sheen, fine horizontal scratches and industrial wear, clean oil finish, seamless tiling, flat orthographic view.
Save as: steel.png

## 2. Tactical Polymer Texture
Prompt: Tactical black polymer plastic texture, matte finish, fine stippled grip pattern, rugged military-grade composite material, seamless tiling, flat orthographic view.
Save as: polymer.png

## 3. Weapon Wood Texture
Prompt: Dark walnut wood grain texture, polished semi-gloss finish, deep brown colors with fine linear grain, high quality furniture wood for rifle stocks, seamless tiling, flat orthographic view.
Save as: wood.png

## --- CHARACTERS ---

## 4. CT Camo (Counter-Terrorist)
Prompt: Urban digital camouflage pattern, shades of navy blue, dark grey, and charcoal, tactical military fabric texture, seamless tiling, flat orthographic view.
Save as: camo_ct.png

## 5. T Camo (Terrorist)
Prompt: Desert digital camouflage pattern, shades of tan, khaki, and light brown, rugged military canvas texture, seamless tiling, flat orthographic view.
Save as: camo_t.png

---

### Instructions for Installation:

1. Generate the images based on the prompts above.
2. Save or convert each image to the .png format.
3. Place the files in the following directory in your project:
   /mnt/c/Users/Jaymes/Github Repos/browser-strike/assets/textures/

### File Names (Case Sensitive):
- assets/textures/steel.png
- assets/textures/polymer.png
- assets/textures/wood.png
- assets/textures/camo_ct.png
- assets/textures/camo_t.png

Once these files are in place, the game's `TextureGenerator.js` will automatically detect them on the next reload and swap out the simple procedural colors for your high-quality textures!
